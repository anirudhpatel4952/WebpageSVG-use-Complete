Citizenship Preparation Classes

These classes help participants to prepare for the Citizenship test. They cover everything one needs to learn from the Discover Canada Guide.

Discover Canada: The Rights and Responsibilities of Citizenship is used by newcomers to study for the citizenship test. It also contains information about the history of Canada, how our governmant works, symbols of Canada and its regions.
